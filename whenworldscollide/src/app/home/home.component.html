Welcome to When Worlds Collide Pizza

Embark on a culinary journey at When Worlds Collide Pizza, located in the heart of Chicago within the iconic Willis Tower. We're not just serving pizza; we're offering a passport to global flavors, bringing together diverse pizza traditions under one roof.

From the simplicity of a classic Neapolitan Margherita, featuring San Marzano tomatoes and fresh mozzarella, to the hearty indulgence of our Chicago Deep Dish Meat Lover's, layered with savory meats and rich marinara, our menu caters to every palate. Explore the crispy, caramelized edges of our Detroit-Style Veggie pizza, or savor the Mediterranean flair of our thick Sicilian pie.

But the adventure doesn't stop there! Each month, we embark on a "World Pizza Tour," featuring unique and exciting creations inspired by global cuisines. From the comforting Irish Shepherd's Pie Pizza in January to the zesty Mexican Al Pastor in June, there's always something new to discover. Explore our full menu in the Menu section and start planning your culinary adventure at When Worlds Collide Pizza!
